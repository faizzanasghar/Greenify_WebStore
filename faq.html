Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about plant care, delivery, and more.

How often should I water my indoor plants?
Most indoor plants prefer to be watered when the top inch of soil feels dry. However, this varies by plant type. Succulents need less water, while ferns need more moisture.
Do you offer international shipping?
Currently, we only ship within the country to ensure the plants arrive fresh and healthy.
What if my plant arrives damaged?
We take great care in packaging, but accidents happen. If your plant arrives damaged, please contact us within 24 hours with photos, and we will replace it.
Are your plants pet-friendly?
Some plants can be toxic to pets. We have a specific category for "Pet Friendly" plants. Please check the product description for details.
